# FOP Approved White Brand System

## Purpose

This document defines the approved white visual system for the Fuzion Operating Platform.

FOP is a bright, restrained, enterprise-grade application for Buildings Under Protection™.

Operational screens must feel architectural, professional, crisp, and calm.

## Approved Visual Reference

The approved direction is:

- white architectural background
- dark navy typography
- electric royal blue BUILD
- platinum silver ADVISE
- vivid pastel highlighter green PROTECT
- clean Living F mark
- full FOP marketing mark used sparingly
- generous whitespace
- minimal shadows
- no dark green workspace
- no black-heavy dashboard
- no oversized dark hero panels

## Brand Architecture

FOP uses two distinct brand assets.

### Living F Mark

The Living F is the primary application mark.

Use it for:

- sidebar
- favicon and app icon
- building lifecycle state
- Passport progress
- building profile
- compact headers
- mobile app surfaces
- protected-state certification

The Living F preserves the lifecycle structure:

- top arm: ADVISE, platinum silver
- middle/lower arm: BUILD, electric royal blue
- vertical stem: PROTECT, highlighter green

Labels may appear at large sizes. Labels should be hidden in compact usage.

Keep the F clean. Do not add decorative streaks or glow in operational UI.

### Full FOP Marketing Mark

The full FOP mark is reserved for:

- public landing page
- login
- presentations
- marketing
- major Passport covers
- protected-state celebration
- splash screens

Do not place the full illustrated FOP logo repeatedly throughout operational screens.

## Application Usage

Operational screens use the white interface by default:

- page background: pure or warm white
- cards: white
- secondary panels: very light cool grey
- text: deep navy
- borders: subtle cool grey
- shadows: minimal
- lifecycle colour: meaningful and restrained

The sidebar should be white or very light grey. Active items may use a pale electric-blue background.

The top bar should be white with a clean search field, minimal controls, and a subtle bottom border.

Mission Control™ should begin with a concise operational header and KPI cards. It must not use an oversized dark hero panel.

## Marketing Usage

The full FOP marketing mark does not use an oversized O or decorative halo.

Official copy:

- FUZION
- OPERATING PLATFORM™
- BUILD • ADVISE • PROTECT
- Buildings Under Protection™
- Projects end. Buildings remain.

Marketing presentation should remain white, crisp, and professional.

## Halo Rules

The Halo™ represents continuous protection, building lifecycle, Buildings Under Protection™, stewardship, and executive intelligence.

In operational product screens, the coral Halo appears only when protected-state certification has been approved by the authoritative rules.

Do not use halo decoration elsewhere.

## White-Interface Rule

The operating application is a white workspace.

Dark poster treatments, black-heavy dashboards, and dark green workspaces are prohibited.

## Prohibited Treatments

Do not use:

- dark green application canvases
- black-heavy dashboards
- oversized dark hero panels in Mission Control™
- repeated full FOP marketing logos in operational screens
- rainbow gradients
- decorative halo effects outside approved protected-state or marketing contexts
- yellow-lime flooding across large panels
- replacement lifecycle names for BUILD • ADVISE • PROTECT
