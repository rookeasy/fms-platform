# The FOP Constitution

Version 1.0

## Our Purpose

"To leave every building better protected than we found it."

FOP exists to preserve, organize, and continuously improve the knowledge required to protect buildings throughout their entire lifecycle.

Projects are temporary.

Buildings endure.

## Our Mission

Buildings Under Protection™

Success is not measured only by projects completed.

Success is measured by buildings that remain protected.

Every building entrusted to FOP becomes part of a permanent digital legacy.

## Our Operating Framework

BUILD • ADVISE • PROTECT is the external lifecycle framework of FOP.

### BUILD

Create Systems.

We design.

We engineer.

We construct.

We commission.

We deliver.

### ADVISE

Deliver Intelligence.

We document.

We analyze.

We validate.

We learn.

We recommend.

Knowledge becomes guidance.

### PROTECT

Sustain Operations.

We inspect.

We maintain.

We repair.

We improve.

Protection is never finished.

## Our Beliefs

Projects end.

Buildings remain.

Every completed project should create a Building Protection Passport™.

Every inspection should make the building smarter.

Every repair should improve the building.

Every document should strengthen the Passport.

Every interaction should increase owner confidence.

## The Building Protection Passport™

The Passport is the permanent digital record of a protected building.

It grows throughout the life of the building.

It is never finished.

## The Building Library

We do not merely collect documents.

We preserve evidence.

Every drawing.

Every inspection.

Every certificate.

Every warranty.

Every photograph.

Every repair.

Every engineer's letter.

Every decision.

Together, they become the institutional memory of the building.

## Artificial Intelligence

AI never replaces professional judgment.

AI assists.

People approve.

FOP suggests.

Professionals decide.

## Data Integrity

One physical building.

One Protected Property.

One Building Protection Passport™.

Never duplicate history.

Always preserve provenance.

## Design Philosophy

Quiet confidence.

Timeless over trendy.

Clarity over decoration.

Consistency over novelty.

Every colour has meaning.

Every screen has purpose.

The F mark may show lifecycle progress.

The halo must be earned.

## Technology Philosophy

Technology should disappear.

Users should think about buildings, not software.

The platform should feel natural.

Simple.

Predictable.

Reliable.

## Customer Promise

We are not delivering software.

We are delivering confidence.

When a customer opens FOP, they should immediately know:

- where the building stands
- what needs attention
- what has changed
- what should happen next

## Our Measure of Success

Not users.

Not logins.

Not downloads.

Our measure is:

Buildings Under Protection™

Every new Building Protection Passport™ represents another promise fulfilled.

## The FOP Standard

Before any feature is built, ask:

Does it help us BUILD?

Does it help us ADVISE?

Does it help us PROTECT?

If the answer is no, we do not build it.

## The Closing Principle

Projects end.

Buildings remain.

Everything we create should ensure that the knowledge required to protect a building lasts far longer than the project that created it.
